# ADR-003: PostgreSQL as the Database 

## Date:
2025-11-11

## Status:
Accepted

## Context:
Project requires form of a relational database to store and manage data. Free and open-source database is the most suitable choice.

## Decision:
PostgreSQL will be used as the primary database for the project due to its advanced features, efficiency and strong open-source community. At the time of writing this ADR, it's 4th most popular DBMS and its adaptation continues to grow. 

## Consequences:
Due to unknown features and limitations of PostgreSQL, some unexpected issues might arise during development and configuration. The developer will gain hands-on expereience with PostgreSQL, up until know only MSSQL and DB2 databases were used by him.
